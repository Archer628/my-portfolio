Project Overview

TravelMate is a travel planning application that helps users create detailed itineraries, discover hidden gems, and organize all aspects of their trips in one place. The app combines AI recommendations with social features to enhance the travel planning experience.

As the lead UI/UX designer, I focused on simplifying the complex process of trip planning while adding delightful moments throughout the journey. The design emphasizes visual inspiration and easy collaboration for group travel.

Key features include AI-powered destination recommendations, collaborative itinerary building, offline map access, budget tracking, and a discover feed for finding local experiences. The app also integrates with booking platforms for seamless reservation management.

TravelMate has been downloaded over 1 million times and has an average rating of 4.7 stars. The app has helped users plan over 500,000 trips worldwide.

Design Process & Screens

Destination Discovery

The home screen features personalized destination recommendations based on travel preferences, past trips, and trending locations. Beautiful imagery and concise information inspire users to explore new places.

Itinerary Builder

Drag-and-drop functionality makes it easy to organize daily activities. The timeline view provides a clear overview of each day's schedule with timing, location details, and booking confirmations.

Collaborative Planning

Group travel features allow friends and family to contribute to itinerary planning. Shared boards, voting on activities, and real-time updates keep everyone informed and excited about the trip.

Offline Maps

Downloadable maps ensure travelers can navigate even without internet connectivity. Points of interest, saved locations, and itinerary pins are all available offline.

Budget Tracking

Integrated budget tools help travelers track expenses in multiple currencies. Visual summaries and category breakdowns keep spending on track throughout the trip.

Travel Journal
