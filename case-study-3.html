Project Overview

NovaTech Solutions is an emerging technology startup specializing in AI-powered business intelligence solutions. They approached me to create a comprehensive brand identity that would position them as innovative yet approachable in the competitive enterprise software market.

The branding project encompassed everything from logo design and visual identity to corporate stationery, presentation templates, and digital assets. The goal was to create a cohesive brand system that could scale as the company grew.

The resulting identity combines geometric precision with dynamic energy, using a color palette of deep blues and vibrant teals to convey trust and innovation. The logo mark represents data convergence and intelligent insights, with a flexible design system that works across various applications.

The new brand identity was launched at TechCrunch Disrupt 2022, where NovaTech secured $5 million in Series A funding. The brand has since become recognizable in the AI and business intelligence space.

Design Process & deliverables

Logo Design Exploration

Multiple logo concepts were explored, ranging from abstract marks to wordmarks. The final design combines a geometric "N" form with an implied data flow pattern, representing the company's AI-powered analytics capabilities.

Color System

The color palette was developed to balance professionalism with innovation. Deep navy represents stability and trust, while electric teal adds energy and positions NovaTech as a forward-thinking industry leader.

Typography System

A modern sans-serif typeface was selected for its excellent readability and technical feel. The type system includes clear hierarchies for headlines, body text, and interface elements.

Brand Guidelines

Comprehensive brand guidelines were created to ensure consistent application across all touchpoints. The document covers logo usage, color specifications, typography, imagery style, and example layouts.

Marketing Materials

Printed and digital marketing materials were designed including business cards, letterhead, brochures, and presentation templates that maintain brand consistency across all communications.

Digital Applications

The brand identity was applied to digital touchpoints including the company website, social media profiles, email signatures, and advertising templates to ensure a cohesive online presence.
