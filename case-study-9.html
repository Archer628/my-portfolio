Project Overview

MediCare Dashboard is a healthcare management platform designed for clinic administrators and medical staff. The system provides comprehensive oversight of patient appointments, medical records, billing, and staff scheduling in a unified interface.

As the lead UI/UX designer, I worked closely with healthcare professionals to understand their workflows and pain points. The design prioritizes efficiency, accuracy, and accessibility while maintaining strict compliance with healthcare data regulations.

Key features include real-time appointment tracking, patient management, billing integration, inventory monitoring for medical supplies, and comprehensive reporting for practice analytics. The interface is designed to reduce cognitive load for busy healthcare workers.

The dashboard has been implemented in over 200 clinics, resulting in 35% reduction in administrative time and significant improvements in patient satisfaction scores.

Design Process & Screens

Healthcare Workflow Research

Extensive observations and interviews with clinic staff revealed critical pain points including appointment conflicts, inefficient patient check-in processes, and difficulty tracking billing status.

Information Architecture

The information architecture was designed to support multiple user roles - administrators, doctors, nurses, and front desk staff - each with customized views and permissions appropriate to their responsibilities.

Main Dashboard

The main dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of daily operations including today's appointments, pending tasks, alerts, and key performance metrics. Customizable widgets allow users to prioritize relevant information.

Patient Management

Comprehensive patient records are accessible in one view, including contact information, medical history, appointment history, billing status, and notes from healthcare providers.

Staff Scheduling

Visual scheduling tools enable efficient staff allocation, shift management, and conflict detection. The calendar view shows availability at a glance while supporting drag-and-drop adjustments.

Analytics & Reporting

Comprehensive reporting tools provide insights into practice performance, patient demographics, revenue trends, and operational efficiency. Custom reports can be generated and exported for stakeholder review.
