Project Overview

EcoCommerce Platform is a sustainable e-commerce website designed for environmentally-conscious consumers and businesses. The platform connects eco-friendly product manufacturers with customers who prioritize sustainability in their purchasing decisions.

As the lead web designer, I focused on creating an immersive user experience that communicates the brand's commitment to sustainability while maintaining a seamless shopping experience. The design incorporates natural color palettes, organic shapes, and micro-animations that evoke a sense of environmental responsibility.

Key features include a carbon footprint calculator, sustainable product certifications display, and a recommendation engine that suggests eco-friendly alternatives to commonly purchased items. The platform also features a blog section for sustainability education and community engagement.

Since launch, EcoCommerce has facilitated over $2 million in sales for sustainable products and has partnered with over 150 eco-certified vendors. The platform has been recognized by GreenTech Magazine as one of the top 10 sustainable e-commerce experiences.

Design Process & Screens

Research & Discovery

Extensive user research was conducted with environmentally-conscious shoppers to understand their pain points and motivations. Surveys and interviews revealed that users wanted transparency in product sourcing and clear environmental impact metrics.

Information Architecture

The site structure was designed to prioritize product discovery while highlighting sustainability credentials. A multi-faceted filtering system allows users to sort by environmental certifications, carbon footprint, and packaging materials.

Visual Design System

The visual identity uses a palette inspired by nature - forest greens, ocean blues, and warm earth tones. Typography was chosen for readability while conveying a modern, approachable feel that aligns with sustainable living.

Product Page Experience

Each product page prominently displays environmental impact data, including carbon footprint, packaging recyclability, and sourcing transparency. Users can also see similar product recommendations that might have lower environmental impact.

Streamlined Checkout

The checkout process was optimized to reduce cart abandonment while offering carbon offset options at the final step. Users can choose to offset their delivery emissions through verified carbon credit programs.

Mobile Experience

The responsive design ensures a consistent experience across all devices. Mobile users benefit from simplified navigation and touch-optimized interactions for browsing and purchasing sustainable products on-the-go.
